Check out IMPACT ARTS

Learn about the many different career opportunities that exist within the art industry.

Animation

Our animation workshops show kids how to create their own cartoon characters and animate them to come alive. We take our IMPACTers on field trips to several major and local animation studios.

Illustration

Our illustration workshops show kids how to create pictures for magazines, books, and adverstising. We take our IMPACTers on field trips to publishing houses and ad agencies to learn more.

Graphic Design

Our graphic design workshops show our IMPACTers how to use Adobe creative suite tools to develop amazing graphic designs. They work side by side with professional graphic designers and learn the creative skills 1-on-1.

Videography

Our videography workshops equipt our IMPACTers with video production skills that will allow them to explore the creative avenues in film production. We take them on field trips to several amazing production studios.
